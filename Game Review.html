Grounded Game Review

Summary

Grounded is a game based on the movie series, Honey I Shrunk The Kids, in the game you are a teenager shrunk down to the size of about an ant, you then have to survive in the backyard by killing insects and animals, collecting resources and exploring, you where shrunk by a company named Ominent, you have to explore and find different labs, such as the Hedge Lab, Koi Pond Lab, Haze Lab and Black Anthill Lab, these labs where created by Ominent, you have to explore and collect memory chips to give to a robot namd BURG.L, he needs these memory due to an explosion he was in causing him to lose his memory, once you have given BURG.L all of his the main 4 memory chips he needs he gives you to embiggening cell recipe, after getting this recipe you need a grilled science, obtained from finding Dr. Wendell Tully, a scientist who used to work for Ominent but quit due to the tests they where doing to children, after defeating the Mant, Dr. Wendell Tully's creation you can find him and obtain the grilled science, once you have it and a few other materials you can go to the Javamatic and make the embiggening cell and beat Director Schemctor you can yourself big again and expose Director Schmector, the head of Ominent, for the things he was doing to children, if you dont beat Director Schmector you get the bad ending where he keeps getting away with what he is doing and Dr. Wendell Tully doesnt get recognition for his work.
